# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers, product leaders, and operators in fintech and adjacent industries who want a clear read on how Daniel thinks about systems, software, and AI-assisted work. Visitors arrive from referrals, social links, or blog posts, usually on desktop or mobile during a break between meetings.

## Product Purpose

A personal site and writing surface for Daniel Zuloaga: introduce who he is, share essays on product and systems, and make it easy to start a conversation. Success looks like a visitor understanding his perspective quickly, reading something useful, or sending a note that leads to real work.

## Brand Personality

Direct, curious, grounded. Voice is plain language over polish, specificity over pitch. The site should feel like a person wrote it, not a template.

## Anti-references

SaaS landing page clichés (hero metrics, identical icon cards, gradient text). AI-default personal branding (uppercase eyebrows on every section, glass cards, IBM Plex / Inter stacks). Cream-and-slate warmth-by-default palettes.

## Design Principles

1. Show the person, not the persona: real copy, real photo, real writing.
2. Hierarchy through type scale and weight, not decorative labels.
3. One surface treatment, varied rhythm: spacing and typography carry structure.
4. Accessible by default: contrast, focus, and form errors are not optional polish.
5. Fast and quiet: minimal JS, no decorative motion gates.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Ensure keyboard focus is visible on all interactive elements. Form errors must associate with fields.
